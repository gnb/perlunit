Updating the website
--------------------

(not yet tested...)

  ssh -a -x $SFLOGIN@perlunit.sourceforge.net

  export CVS_RSH=ssh
  export CVSROOT=:ext:`whoami`@cvs.PerlUnit.sourceforge.net:/cvsroot/perlunit
  cd /home/groups/p/pe/perlunit/
  cvs exp -r HEAD www
  chmod -R g+w www
  mv htdocs htdocs~   ; mv www/htdocs .
# mv cgi-bin cgi-bin~ ; mv www/cgi-bin .

I'm sure you can improvise on the theme...


If you're just updating the index, paste this instead:
(also not tested in paste mode)
  cd /home/groups/p/pe/perlunit/
  cvs exp -r HEAD www/htdocs/index.php
  chmod g+w www/htdocs/index.php
